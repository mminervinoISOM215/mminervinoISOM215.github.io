    Company


    McCarthy's Cafe is a brand new business located in the heart of Boston. We are an Irish cafe that offers true Irish culture and brings a little bit of Ireland to Boston. With such a diverse city, I saw the oppurtunity to open a laid back place for people to come and relax and immerse in the experience. We offer real Irish treats and food inspired by what you would find walking around the streets back home.
